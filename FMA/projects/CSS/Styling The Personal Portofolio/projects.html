What Are The Most Spoken Languages in the World?

Ever wonder what the most widely spoken languages in the world are? Although there are currently more than 7,000 languages, more than half of the world's population speaks just 23 of them.

Top 5 Most Spoken Languages in the World are:

  1. English - 1,456M
  2. Chinese (Mandarin) - 1,138M
  3. Hindi - 609.5M
  4. Spanish - 559.1M
  5. French - 308.9M
